Travel Guide in the Known Universe of STAR WARS

Welcome to this travel guide about the Known Universe in Star Wars, home to 3.2 million planets, although about 45 are shown in the movies. Hopefully, this travel guide would give you a quick glimpse of how diverse this universe, the dangerous, and the beauty it has.

Coruscant

The Capital of the Known Universe in Star Wars, Coruscant is a city-scaped planet that is home to roughly 2 trillion people, humans and other sentients alike.

If you love the city, then Coruscant is the place to go. However, be wary of how large the planet is. Deep underground, is where the underworld is and it's very dangerous there, somewhere tourists definitely should not check out. Even the Jedi don't have much power and reign there.

Kashyyyk

Home of the Wookie species, Kashyyyk is covered in a dense forest of Worsyhr Trees, trees that can grow up 1,000 feet tall and live for 50,000 years.

Although a beautiful place, Wookies have a history of being enslaved, so they are very wary of foreigners. Wookies are also multiple times stronger than the average human, with an average height of 7'2", and can live for over 400 years.

Tatooine

Welcome to the harsh, desert planet of Tatooine within the Outer Rim of the Known Universe. Tatooine orbits a twin star system, creating a dynamic and a must-see sunset. Tatooine is also home to two native species, the Jawas and the Tuskens. Jawas are scavengers, so they don't pose much of a danger, however Tuskens are known as Tusken Raiders and they are known to be vicious, so always watch where you sleep at night!

Tatooine is located in the Outer Rim, where it is out of reach by any government, causing the planet to be a lawless place filled with runaways, criminals, and smugglers. And do be careful of the Hutt Clan, the major power on Tatooine. If your not careful and get on the wrong side of the Hutts, you might end up getting chased by bounty hunters and get frozen in carbonite, in a state of living, yet frozen.

STAR WARS

The Known Universe in Star Wars is not for the light hearted! But if you adhere to the warnings in this travel guide, and find the right help, then you should be OK. This isn't a survival guide, but was made to show you some of the planets here in the Known Universe, and the beauties and dangerous of the planets. Remember, the Known Universe is a very big place, as there are over millions of planets with stranger things going on their surface. Don't get turned away by the Hutts or the Imperials or the occasion space pirates! If you look hard enough you will eventually find a nice, safe haven where you can take your family and rest peacefully.

With that being said, I hope you found this Travel Guide in the Known Universe of Star Wars to be helpful in your search for the perfect travel destination!
